Fresh food logistics

Fliit is an early-stage startup building a platform to connect fresh food shipments with carriers. The prioritized focus is simplifying the carrier workflow by automating shipment routing, invoicing, and payment. I partnered with the team to deliver a minimum viable product of the carrier platform.

Outline of the product's long term vision

One interaction is required from the user: upload his/her tour excel spreadsheet
Template includes required field for tour, driver, shipment, and payment details.
Once data upload is complete user may then edit info and submit for payment (when tour state is complete). Data may be reused for future tours (later phase)
UI has consistent selection pattern, easier learnability for user and quicker front-end development time
